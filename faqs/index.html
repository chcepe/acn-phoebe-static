FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions from our participants!

Is Demystifying AI a pre-requisite of AI Engineering School?

No, those are separate trainings. Demystifying AI is a 1 day training intended for ARCP leads with the goal of making the participants understand the fundamentals of AI. AI Engineering School is a 2-week course intended for career levels 12 to 7 and is a more technical training. It aims to give the participants an idea of how to create an AI application by using some AI tools, libraries, APIs, etc.

What are the minimum skills needed before I register to the AI trainings?

You should have at least a knowledge of OOP, Basic Python and Basic Statistics. This is for AI Engineering School. There is no pre-requisite for Demystifying AI since this is more of an overview of AI.

When is Local AI hackathon held?

The local AI hackathon is held every quarter of a fiscal year. Stay tuned for the announcements from our Training Admin.

What WBS will be used for all of the trainings in AI?

For trainings, we shall use the Local Training WBS in myTE (730X93).

How can I register to the AI trainings?

You can register to the AI trainings via myLearning.accenture.com. The postcard sent by the training admin should also have the link to the registration.

To whom do I reach out to in case I need to cancel my training?

For cancellations, replacements, etc., you may reach out to our training admin.

Can I take a leave during the training?

Yes, you may take a leave as long as you fulfill the minimum requirement of the training. That is, a participant shall attend at least 80% of the session to be credited in myLearning.

Is there a chance that I will become part of the AI team after taking the training?

There is a chance but it is not guaranteed. There are a couple of factors to be considered for a resource to be able to go to AI. Is the resource locked to a project? Does he/she performed well in the training? Is there a demand? and a lot more factors. But you may update your skills in skills.accenture.com to highlight your preferences.
